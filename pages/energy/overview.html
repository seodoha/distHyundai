  • Panama

    2-stroke Diesel Engine Power Plant

    • High Efficiency
    • Low OPEX
    • EPC

    In March 2011, Autoridad del Canal de Panama(ACP) placed an order to HHI-EMBU for engineering, procurement and construction of stationary power plant consisting of two sets of HYUNDAI-MAN 12K80MC-S engines in order to supply continuous power to Panama Canal and national grid in Panama. This 2-stroke diesel engine power plant has accommodated the fundamental demands for lower operating cost with less replacement parts and longer exchange periods.

    • Total Output70MV
    • CustomerACP
    • Operating ModeContinuous
    • Gensets12K80MC - S x 2sets

    • ScopeEPC

  • Cuba

    Packaged Power Station
    & Diesel Power Plant

    • Energy Revolution
    • Rapid Construction
    • 30% of Cuba Electricity

    In the mid 2000s, Cuba experienced chronic electricity shortages resulting in frequent power outages. To deal with the energy crisis, it set up a plan of upgrading its power infrastructure based on a contract with a foreign supplier. While many companies gave up the project, HYUNDAI eventually won the deal in 2005 and met urgent requirements of Cuba with 576MW Packaged power station and 310MW Diesel power plant across the country. For the first time in history, a company product was illustrated on country's currency.

    • Total Output576MV(PPS)310MW(DPP)
    • CustomerEnergo Import
    • Operating ModeContinuous
    • Gensets9H21/32 x 340sets 9H25/33 x 124sets
    • FuelHFO
    • ScopeEP
    • Delivered2005~2009

  • Colombia

    Dual Fuel Engine Power Plant

    • Rapid Construction
    • Reduce Emission
    • EPC

    The biggest dual fuel engine power plant in Colombia In February 2017, Hyundai received an order from TERMONORTE S.A.S E.S.P., for engineering, procurement and construction. The contract consists of 10 sets of HiMSEN dual fuel engine generator to supply continuous power to national grid in Colombia, South America. The power plant was handed over in November 2018 to the customer and is currently under commercial operation.

    • Total Output93MV
    • CustomerTERMONORTE
    • Operating ModeBase Load
    • Gensets20H35DFV x 10sets
    • FuelNatural Gas, Diesel Oil, Heavy Fuel Oil
    • ScopeEPC
    • Delivered2018.11
