Digitalization

Digitalization for efficiency, flexibility and profitability

Journey of disruptive innovation HHI-EMD is fundamentally changing its products
and services, working and business models
through digital transformation.
Since the development of Internet technology and the spread of personal smart devices have been a catalyst, Overall changes are taking place. HHI-EMD builds a smart factory by applying the Internet of Things (IoT) and information and communication technology (ICT) to the manufacturing process. We provides highly reliable and highly efficient customized products and services through predictive diagnosis management and digital control technology.
