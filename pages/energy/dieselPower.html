Why Are
They Good?
1. Fuel Flexibility

HYUNDAI's diesel engine power plant provides a variety of selection of fuels, ranging from HFO, LFO, Crude oil to Emulsified oil.

2. Quick Start Time

Diesel engines have a shorter start time compared to turbines. It takes up to 15 to 40 minutes for turbines to start, whereas diesel engine only takes 2 minutes.

3. High Reliability

We provide robust, reliable engine generator set and auxiliary equipment, which are proven in the most challenging nations and environmental conditions.
