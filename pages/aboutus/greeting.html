Thank you for Visiting
the Hyundai
Heavy Industries’ Engine &
Machinery Division

Despite difficulties from the global economic crisis, we accomplished 100 million bhp in 2-stroke diesel engines production in the shortest time in the world. Our HiMSEN engine adopted as the emergency generator for nuclear power plant for the first time.

First, we will obtain the growth engines for new product development and new business launch. By focusing on new models and product development, we will continuously expand joinable market. Fuel gas supply system NoNOx SCR Syatem and ballast water management systems, we will increase sales through promoting new business.

Second, we will strive to maintain the quality of our products and reduce their costs. We will work on existing products for zero-defects by improving design and quality management, and new products for early stabilization of quality by standardizing process methods.

We will also study cost analysis and design improvements to remain competitive for small & medium size bore 2-stroke engines and HiMSEN engines. I guarantee that we can achieve our business objects this year if we always try to find better way and work with challenging attitude.

Additionally, I request that we all observe the safety rules and focus on preventing accidents at work so that we can make a zero-accidents workplace.

I wish all of you health and happiness.

An Kwang-hean COO & Senior Executive Vice President Engine & Machinery
