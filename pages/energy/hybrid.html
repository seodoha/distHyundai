BOGOTA / 4.5MW HYBRID POWER PLANT
Hybrid Power Plant with Gas Engine and Solar Panels through Korea’s Official Development Assistance Program

Hyundai Heavy Industries’ first hybrid power plant will be built in 2022. This plant is comprised of a 3.5MW gas engine and 1MW solar panels to dispatch stable electricity in Bogota, the capital city of Colombia. This contract was originated from Korea’s Official Development Assistance program through several years’ investigation and interaction between Korean and Colombian governments to encourage domestic manufacturing industry and contribute to Colombia’s economic development with living standard.
